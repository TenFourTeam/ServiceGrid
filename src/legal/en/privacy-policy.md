## 1. Information We Collect

Describing data gathered.

- Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and address when you register.
- Business Information: Company name, team members, customer data you enter.
- Payment Information: Processed securely via Stripe; we do not store card details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Explaining our data purposes.

- Provide and improve ServiceGrid services.
- Process payments and send invoices.
- Communicate updates, support, and marketing (opt-out available).

## 3. Data Sharing

Clarifying third-party access.

- Stripe for payment processing.
- Google Maps for location and routing services.
- We do not sell your personal data.

## 4. Data Security

Protecting your information.

- Encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.
- Regular security audits and access controls.
- Incident response within 72 hours of breach detection.

## 5. Your Rights

Empowering user control.

- Access, correct, or delete your data via settings or privacy@servicegrid.com.
- Export your data at any time.
- Opt-out of marketing communications.

## 6. Data Retention

Specifying storage duration.

- Active account data retained while you use ServiceGrid.
- Deleted accounts: Data removed within 30 days, except as required by law.

## 7. Contact Us

Reaching our privacy team.

- Email: privacy@servicegrid.com
- Address: Available upon request.
